Gear you'll need

Helmet

The most important. Donâ€™t compromise on this.

Lock

General rule is the heavier the lock, the harder to cut through. D-locks are favourable. Even better use a D-lock for the frame and a cable lock to secure the wheels. (Kryptonite do a great package). Make sure you secure your front wheel as these clip out easily and so often get stolen around cities.

Lights

The more of a lightbulb you look like, the better. You can get lights in any cycling shop, in fact you can even get them in Wilko on the cheap.

High-Vis

As above, think flashing lightbulb theme.

Backback cover

If you really hate High Vis, at least get a high-vis backpack cover. You can use this instead. And plus it protects your backpack and its contents from rain.
